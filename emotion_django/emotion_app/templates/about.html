About EmotionAI

Technology

EmotionAI is powered by a deep learning Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained to recognize seven distinct human emotions: Angry, Disgust, Fear, Happy, Sad, Surprise, and Neutral.

The model has been trained on thousands of facial expression images and uses advanced computer vision techniques to detect faces and classify emotions with high accuracy.

How It Works

  1. Face Detection: Uses Haar Cascade Classifier to detect faces in images or video frames
  2. Preprocessing: Converts detected faces to grayscale and resizes to 48x48 pixels
  3. Emotion Classification: Deep learning model analyzes facial features
  4. Results: Displays the detected emotion with confidence scores
